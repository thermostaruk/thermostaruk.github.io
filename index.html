The Science

Hygienic Cleanliness at 174°C

Thanks to Thermostar technology, steam is heated in the boiler to up to 174°C before cleaning begins. This produces exceptionally dry steam — perfectly suited for textiles, carpets and upholstery without leaving moisture stains or destroying impregnation.

The Thermostar heating elements deliver 28W/cm² — four times more than standard spiral heating elements. The self-decalcifying design means lime deposits fall off as the element expands with heat, ensuring consistent performance for years.

  • Germs and bacteria are killed
  • Greases dissolved without chemicals
  • Impregnation preserved on treated textiles
  • Ideal for allergy sufferers — reduces mites

Under the Hood

Thermostar Technology

More than 100,000 units sold worldwide. The technology in Thermostar is now used in many other products globally.

True Temp Technology

The electromagnetic float in Thermostar ensures high-quality, dry steam throughout the entire service life. Unlike electronic sensors that calcify over time, True Temp stays accurate.

Self-Decalcifying Element

28W/cm² — four times more power than standard elements. The heating element expands with temperature, causing limescale deposits to fall off automatically.

Two-Chamber System

The steam cleaner can be refilled at any time, even at full operating temperature — no cooling down period needed. Never wait to top up.

AISI 304 Stainless Boiler

Produced exclusively from AISI 304 stainless steel — withstands up to 600°C. Welded via Tungsten Inert Gas process and pressure-tested to 70 bar.
