Why Thermostar?

A Product That
Sells Itself

Thermostar is not available in retail stores. It is sold exclusively through authorised distributors — which means no price competition, no race to the bottom, and a customer base that comes directly to you.

With steam at up to 190°C, no chemicals, and a lifelong restoration warranty, one live demonstration is all it takes. Customers see it work on their own home and they buy.

  • Sold exclusively through distributors — no retail competition
  • Live demonstration converts at exceptionally high rates
  • Lifelong warranty creates trust and removes hesitation
  • Repeat business through accessories and referrals
  • Growing consumer demand for chemical-free cleaning
  • Environmentally positioned product — increasingly relevant

The Process

How It Works

Thermostar is a direct-sales model. You arrange home demonstrations with interested customers, show the product in action in their own home, and close the sale on the spot. No shop needed. No stock room. Just you, the product, and the customer.

The demonstration does the selling — customers watch steam dissolve grease, clean their carpet, and sanitise their mattress without a single chemical. It speaks for itself.
